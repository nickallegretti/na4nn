Instructions:

1) Make Sure You Sign In!

You cannot upload your character preset to the server unless you sign into your google account.

2) Customize Your Character

Personalize your character to your liking. You must change the character from the preset to upload it.

3) Give Your Character a Name and a Description

Your character must have a name and description to be uploaded as a preset.

4) Upload Your Character

Thats it. Now your character is a preset viewable by other users.
